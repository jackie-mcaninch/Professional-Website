About Me

In the future of my career, I hope to work with algorithms for big data sets, because I love analyzing patterns and solving problems with efficient solutions. My greatest interests in this sector are applications in finance, trading, and cryptocurrencies; I am always excited to learn more about these topics! Currently, I am looking for internships and research opportunities for the summer of 2021, so that I can gain the real-world experience I need to succeed in my career later in life.

These people have influenced me in so many ways to make me the person I am today, and it is my goal to have the same impact on everyone I meet.
